Find a Chapter

Join APMP Find a Chapter

APMP has 28 chapters throughout the world.

Find the closest chapter to where you live or network with other chapters by searching below. View their websites, and see with who you want to affiliate and network.

Can't Find a Chapter Near You?

Consider starting your own APMP chapter. All you need is:

  • A minimum of 15 industry professionals from a variety of companies
  • A desire to start up your own APMP chapter
  • Be a part of a global community of APMP chapters

Learn how to start a new APMP chapter

Chapter Listing

 

The APMP ANZ Chapter serves all proposal professionals in Australia and New Zealand. The chapter also serves members in the Asia Pacific region in locations including Singapore and Hong Kong. The ANZ Chapter was formed in 2008 and is a vibrant and growing chapter, dedicated to increasing recognition of the value of professional proposal development.

Chapter Website: https://www.apmpanz.org/
   

The APMP Carolinas Chapter was founded in 1998 and serves the North and South Carolina region. The chapter meets at least four times a year with dinner programs and speakers. The Carolinas Chapter is nationally recognized as an "outstanding" chapter for its active and consistent support of APMP membership events and activities. The Chapter also co-hosts the successful Southern Proposal Accents Conference (SPAC), which has been held annually for more than 10 years and draws participants from across the country and internationally.

Chapter Website: https://www.apmpcarolinas.org/
   

The Chesapeake Chapter of APMP provides a broad range of opportunities to Bid, Proposal, Business Development, Capture, and Graphics Professionals throughout Maryland and the Greater Mid-Atlantic region.

Coupled with the goals and objectives of APMP, Chesapeake Chapter strives to promote better business development, acquisition, and education focused on our members, our members’ organizations, and to improve members’ professional knowledge and skills. Our focus is on Community Engagement, Professional Development & Training and broader Education opportunities, as well as professional certification thru the APMP Certification Program.

Chapter Website:  www.chesapeakeapmp-fr.online

For general information, upcoming programs and events, membership information or other questions – please contact us at: [email protected]
   

The Colorado Chapter of APMP that serves the Rocky Mountain Region has approximately 300 members from across Colorado, Utah, Wyoming, Montana, Idaho, and the Intermountain West. The chapter delivers a variety of engagement opportunities and educational content to our members. We host monthly webinars on a variety of proposal-related topics, periodic in-person events in our hub locations, opportunities to work toward your next level certification, and offer various tools and support to help our members thrive within the industry. Named the 2021-2022 Chapter of the Year, we welcome all members to join our webinars and encourage you to inquire about our chapter initiatives.

Contact: [email protected]

APMP Costa Rica is the youngest chapter of APMP, the global industry body, and community for bid and proposal management professionals. It was formed in Jan 2023 and is dedicated to increasing recognition of Bid & Proposal Professionals in the Latin American region.

As a group of like-minded professionals and companies, we will promote and share proven techniques, tools, and experiences with and amongst our members. As a multilingual chapter, we plan to host regular friendly networking events in English, Spanish, and Portuguese, covering interesting and relevant subjects, from how to engage with current procurement practice to the art and science of pitching.

The APMP Florida Sunshine Chapter was chartered in 2007. Membership does not require Florida residency to be a chapter member. Although most of the chapter's affiliated members are in Florida, it welcomes chapter affiliation and involvement of APMP members anywhere, particularly in nearby states that have no chartered APMP chapters. The Florida Sunshine Chapter chapter is virtual. Members can attend its four annual general membership meetings via computer and telephone.

Chapter Website: https://apmpflorida.org/

Chartered in 1994, the APMP Georgia Peach State Chapter has earned a reputation as an innovative, active local organization. Its members come from Atlanta, other parts of Georgia, and neighboring states. The chapter holds a combination of virtual and dinner meetings in Atlanta and has co-hosted the Southern Proposal Accents Conference (SPAC). Chapter events include valuable educational programs and an opportunity to network with peers in the proposal profession. The chapter welcomes any proposal professional from the Southeastern US to participate in its active program and affiliate with its active membership.

Chapter Website: https://www.apmp-ga.com/

The chapter hosts monthly webinars featuring a range of topics related to all aspects of capture and proposal management. APMP UK is the UK chapter of the Association of Bid Proposal Management Professionals (APMP), founded in 2001. Through association and education, the chapter provides members with access to tools, methods, processes, innovations, talent and specialised expertise that can directly improve the ability to acquire new business and to sustain growth and competitiveness. The chapter meets regularly to listen to a selection of business winning experts from a variety of backgrounds, presenting on a multitude of topics. The flagship event is the annual conference held in October, which has become the must-attend event for any business winning professional in Europe serious about advancing knowledge and skills from some of the finest experts from across the world.

Chapter Website: https://www.apmpuk.co.uk/
 

Established on July 15, 2021, the APMP Western Chapter was formed from a merger of the APMP Valley of the Sun Chapter and the APMP California Chapter, both chapters with long histories. The Valley of the Sun Chapter was the very first APMP chapter to be chartered, founded by the late Howard Nutt, CF APMP Fellow, in Phoenix, Arizona, in 1991, only two years after APMP itself was founded. The California Chapter was formed in 2012 from the merger of the Northern California (NorCal) and Southern California (SoCal) chapters: the NorCal chapter grew from the Golden Gate chapter, APMP Chapter No. 6, chartered in 1994 and the SoCal chapter was chartered in 1996 as APMP Chapter No. 10.

The APMP Western Chapter serves all members in Hawaii, California, Nevada, Arizona, and New Mexico.

Chapter Website: apmp-western.org
