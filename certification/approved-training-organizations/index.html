The Foundation Level exam fee does not include payment for Certification training. ATO training to prepare for the Foundation level exam costs approximately $195 to $500 USD per person, depending on the vendor and training format.

APMP values our network of Approved Training Organizations and Approved Trainers and the contribution they make to promoting best practices and championing professional development in bid and proposal management. APMP welcomes applications from professional training organizations looking to achieve Approved status and suitably experienced and qualified trainers wishing to join our worldwide network. For more information about becoming an APMP Approved Training Organization, please download the APMP Intellectual Property Agreement and visit the APMG website.
