About APMP Fellows

Get to Know Our APMP Fellows

 

The APMP Fellows award is one of our association's highest individual honors. It recognizes individuals who have made significant contributions to the bid and proposal industry throughout their careers.

The award is open to all APMP members in good standing who have belonged to the association for five or more years.

Selected nominees are judged and are inducted into the Fellows class once a year during APMP's Honors Gala. Only members can nominate a Fellow.
