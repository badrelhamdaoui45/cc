BPC Barcelona Speakers

Event Home

Emilia Dronkert, CF APMP - Proposal Manager at Amazon Web Services

Emilia is a public sector Proposal Manager for Amazon Web Services, Inc. (AWS), where she has been since July 2020. Prior to joining AWS, she was a Contracts Negotiator for Lockheed Martin. Emilia has a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science, Magna Cum Laude, from Seattle University and is Foundation certified through APMP. In her free time, she enjoys skiing, traveling, and horseback riding.

Amy Aiello - Senior Bid Manager at IRG Advisors LLP

Amy is the senior bid manager at an executive search firm, having started her career as the bid coordinator/receptionist at a contractor. In addition to Amy's core role, she runs bid and presentation training internally, as well as presenting at company away days, team away days, APMP UK, and Europe.

Jim Carley - Managing Director at Carley Consult Ltd

Jim Carley has 25 years of experience in bid writing and bid management and has managed his own consultancy firm in this space since 2007. His business, Carley Consult, won the inaugural APMP Supplier of the Year Award in 2021, and he is a former presenter at both BIDx UK and BPC Europe. He is also the Vice President of Doncaster Chamber of Commerce, and his bid writing accolades include writing Doncaster's winning bid to achieve city status through the UK's Platinum Jubilee Civic Honour's Competition in 2022.

Laxman Murugappan - Founder of PlayThinkTransform

Laxman is a Lead Coach, Mentor & Principal Consultant in Enterprise BPM, Customer Experience Design, Customer Journey Mapping at BPGroup/Academy of Customer Experience. He has been providing mentoring & implementation advisory to Startups, SME's in areas of Empathy Mapping, Value Proposition Canvas,Lean Startup Canvas, Business Model Canvas, Design Thinking,User Experience, User centred design. Laxman is also certified in Customer Experience Management, Lego Serious Play Facilitation , TOGAF v. 9.1, HFI- Certified Usability Analyst (centring around User Centred Design, Design in IT systems), ITIL-V3. 

Kati Schoenwitz - Proposal Manager At Burns & McDonnell

Kati is a marketing and creative communications specialist with a background in value-added content development and exceptional client experiences. She is APMP and Shipley certified in proposal management best practices. Kati specializes in designing attractive, easy-to-evaluate proposals and incorporating client-focused strategic messaging to help improve win and capture rates.

Jon Williams - Managing Director at Strategic Proposals

As Managing Director of Strategic Proposals, Jon has helped clients in over 35 countries win major deals and transform their bid and proposal processes. A Fellow of APMP, he's given keynote speeches and conference presentations around the world.

Jon's always stayed close to the world of procurement, where he started his career. He's looking forward to sharing the findings of this extensive research project, carried out in autumn 2023. 

Susan Hanning, CP APMP - Senior Consultant at Strategic Proposals Ltd

Susan Hanning, CP APMP began her career as a research psychologist, before moving, via marketing, into the world of proposals. This background allows her to offer fascinating new perspectives into the work that we do and how it is received by our audiences. Susan led the proposal team for a large, global insurer before moving to Strategic Proposals in 2013. Here, she splits her time between supporting live deals and delivering acclaimed (and unique) training courses on both proposal writing and sales presentations for clients around the world. She is a confident and engaging presenter who thrives on helping others to enhance what they do.

Marly Theunisse, CF APMP - Director at Guidehouse

Marly Theunisse is a proposal manager from the Netherlands. She has 12 years of proposal experience and is APMP Foundation and Micro-Graphics certified. Marly has 18 years of experience in the energy consulting sector and 20 years in overall ESG/sustainability. In her current role as Director at Guidehouse, Marly leads the Europe / Middle East proposal team, and she is part of the global Guidehouse proposal leadership team. For the past 1,5 years, Marly has worked at the global company level on in-house ESG reporting, among others on a proposal FAQ on sustainability questions.

Marly holds a Master (2001) in Spanish language. Within this study, her core tracks were international relations and intercultural communication. As part of this MA, she attended a full year at Madrid University Complutense, tracking ‘communication and journalism’. In 2003-2004 Marly did a post-doc at CEDLA University (Amsterdam) on ‘Latin America studies – Sustainable development’. Her study topic was sustainable (Fair Trade) coffee, most notably about and within Mexico. She did a 3 months field study in Oaxaca, including extensive on-site work at coffee farms, coffee cooperatives and factories. Marly wrote her 2 master theses fully in Spanish. In September 2006, Marly published an online article in Dutch about the certification (variety and types) of sustainable coffee, after completing her freelance research assignment for UTZ Kapeh (now part of Rainforest Alliance). During this same time, starting in 2005, she joined the renewable energy consultancy company “Ecofys” where she also gained ESG knowledge on energy, next to coffee. Since 2005 she has practically always been working in the renewable/sustainable energy sector in different roles and settings. Since early 2012 Marly has been a proposal manager, continuously building up her knowledge.

Gareth Earle CP APMP - Senior Consultant at BidCraft

Gareth is the technical author of PAS 360, the first formal bidding standard. He has 14+ years of experience in bidding, managing individual opportunities and bidding functions.

He's a regular speaker at conferences. In 2022, he spoke in Amsterdam, Dallas and London on topics including how to influence the evaluators and make the big stuff in the bidding world feel human-sized. Gareth also served as APMP UK Chapter Board CEO in 2020.

Gareth’s motto? ‘Rocket science isn’t rocket science to rocket scientists.’ That’s why he loves making the complex simple for clients and pointing people in the right direction to succeed. He transfers his knowledge and leaves people with the tools they need to understand how to help themselves. It’s a legacy thing.

Daniel Heuman - CEO and Creator of PerfectIt

Daniel Heuman is the CEO and creator of PerfectIt. He developed the software to give users a faster and more accurate way of handling mechanical editing tasks. His software, PerfectIt, is used by bids and proposals teams across the globe.

Susanne Jakob - Team Lead Proposal Management at adesso SE

Susanne leads a Proposal Management Team at adesso SE, a Germany-based and fast-growing IT Consulting company with 10,000+ employees, and is a certified intercultural trainer and conflict mediator. For over 10 years, she worked in a Consulting firm with a focus on cross-cultural collaboration and internationalization where she built up and led the geographically dispersed Proposal Management Team. She holds a Master's degree in Peace and Conflict Studies (Goethe-University of Frankfurt) and a Bachelor's degree in International Cultural and Business Studies (University of Passau). Susanne brings a positive attitude toward cultural diversity as well as her multifaceted experience as a mediator and proposal manager.

Katie Flood - Sr. Manager, Content at Loopio

With a background in journalism and communications, Katie Flood is a seasoned storyteller with 10+ years of industry experience.

Today, she leads the content team at Loopio, where she is responsible for publishing the annual RFP trends & benchmarks report, which surveys RFP teams around the globe.

Vicky Hampson - Consulting Partner at Defy Expectations Ltd.

A dynamic, data-driven, insight-led senior leader with demonstrated capabilities in designing and implementing solutions to solve complex business problems via effective processes, systems, and workflows. Vicky is accomplished in facilitating effective cross-functional collaboration across Sales, Marketing, and Product teams via effective Go-To-Market strategies.

With a history of proven senior leadership and a strong coaching and mentoring capability, she has worked with diverse Global teams and cultures to identify and overcome complex challenges, lead teams to accomplish goals and deliver commercial results, and impact consistently in businesses and institutions of all sizes and maturity, ranging across Global enterprise organisations, Start Up and Scale Up companies and Public institutions.

Christopher Farnath - Founder and Executive Coach at Service Instinct Ltd.

Christopher has over 25 years of experience in the B2B SaaS sector, specialising in customer success and engagement. He has worked in global leadership roles, leading teams across six different continents, Christopher provides advice and business mentorship to build teams and transform their customer experience. He is an expert in technology services and has worked for leading brands in the cloud contact centre, healthcare, education, retail and transport industries, saving £ millions whilst improving customer outcomes.

Pippa Birch, CAP CPP APMP - Company Founder & Bid Consultant at Pipster Solutions Ltd

Pippa Birch is an APMP Professional certified bid consultant and writer of over 20 years. She runs a bidding consultancy in the UK that specialises in highways and civil engineering - Pipster Solutions Ltd. As a supporter of her fellow bid professionals, Pippa is an APMP UK Rapport Mentor and won the APMP UK Contribution to the Profession award in 2022. She hosts the ever-popular Thursday Throng and Bid Bites online sessions, which are provided free for all bid professionals across the globe. In 2021 she had the crazy idea to undertake the 60km Santorini Circuit - by kayak and on foot over one day - to raise money for SANDS. In 2023 she successfully completed it, collecting over 3500 pounds.

Bruce Morton, APMP Fellow - Corporate Capture Director at HII Mission Technologies

Bruce is an APMP Fellow & Capture Management Director at HII Mission Technologies. Prior to that he held Capture Management positions at Lockheed Martin, Raytheon, CACI, and SAIC. Bruce was also a Spacecraft System Design Engineer at GE Aerospace. Concurrently, he taught Graduate Courses in Cybersecurity at George Washington University in Washington, DC. Bruce has been a presenter and panelist at APMP BPC Conferences since 2007.

Danelle Morrow, CF APMP - Vice President, North America Proposal Development Center at Sodexo

Danelle Morrow is a dynamic, growth-minded proposal executive with more than 15 years of experience driving innovation and leading award-winning teams. She’s achieved new business growth and client retention at a myriad of organizations from start-ups to Fortune 100 companies. Danelle has been recognized by APMP as both a 40 Under 40 award winner and a group recipient of the Chapter of the Year award. She currently provides strategic leadership for a global organization, overseeing the North American Proposal Development Center. She manages 80 people across four teams producing over 800 proposals valued at $4B in annual sales.

Len Miller, CF APMP - CEO at Hinz Consulting

Len Miller is the CEO of Hinz Consulting. He works with the Executive Leadership Team to achieve the company’s goals. He directly supports a portfolio of Hinz’s clients in bid pursuits, pipeline analysis, and reviews. He is a frequent webinar/conference presenter on BD, capture and proposal topics and is an expert instructor in these topics. He has over 38 years of experience in Federal/DOD and commercial business development, capture management, proposal management, product support, systems/software engineering, quality assurance, and program management while working at Siemens, CACI, EYT, DynCorp IE&T, SIMTEC, and Delex Systems. He has helped win over $37B since 1990 and has supported 17 $B+ proposals and multiple projects worth $100M+. Len holds a BS degree in Physics from VCU; is a member of the Association of Proposal Management Professionals (APMP) and is a member of the APMP National Capital Area Board.

Amy McGeady, CPP APMP Fellow - Senior VP of Strategic Services at Shipley Associates

Amy McGeady is the Senior Vice President of Strategic Services for Shipley Associates in the US. Amy has been a CEO, a proposal centre director in a Fortune 100 company, a small business owner, and a proposal manager and writer. She is an APMP Fellow and a member of the APMP Board of Directors. Most recently, Amy presented on Microsoft Copilot and the Implications for Proposal Development at the APMP Western Conference and facilitated an expert panel on AI at the 2023 BPC in Orlando.

Mark Taylor, CP APMP - Managing Director at Shipley UK

Mark Taylor is the Managing Director of Shipley UK and brings over 20 years of hands-on experience in supporting European organisations in the aerospace, information technology, and defence sectors to develop their approach to business winning. His focus is always to enthuse people about professional complex business winning and new ways of working.
In addition to his client work, Mark has driven a digital transformation programme within Shipley UK.

Chantell Kimpson - Justice & Immigration Bid Management Director at Serco

Chantell is Serco's Justice & Immigration Bid Management Director and leads her bidding function with a strong focus on work-winning, compassionate leadership and creating environments where teams can thrive, personally and professionally. With over 12 years of bid leadership experience, she has worked for a range of large outsourcing organisations in justice, healthcare, IT and digital in a range of bid roles after beginning her career teaching Law at Durham University.

Jeff Goldfinger, CF APMP - Founder and Educator at Xtra Mile Training and Development

Jeff graduated from Northwestern University with a degree in Computer Science and was immediately commissioned as an Ensign in the U.S. Navy. After a 20-year career in naval aviation, Jeff has spent more than a decade in the aerospace and defense industry. When serving in a capture or proposal role, Jeff has directly won over $50M in new business and consulted/coached teams that have captured more than $1.5Bn. He and his clients have produced an average proposal win rate of 82% in an industry where 30-35% is the norm.

As an instructor/educator / public speaker, Jeff has more than thirty-five years of experience at all training levels – apprentice to expert, vocational to masters-degree credit, small workshops, and weeklong conferences. He has spoken in front of classrooms and to audiences from six to six hundred on five of the seven continents.

Carlo Spellucci - Lead Facilitator South Europe at Rasmussen Consulting

In 2007 he took the position of Head of Procurement at Eurofly then in 2009 he moved to Pharma at Eli Lilly as Lean Six Sigma Black Belt. Carlo worked in continuous improvement projects across the entire pharma value chain until he took the role of Corporate Innovation Champion kicking-off the Design Thinking journey at Eli Lilly and bringing the LEGO SERIOUS PLAY method to Europe, Canada and Japan.

Steven Coles, CPP APMP - Senior Customer Success Manager at Upland Qvidian

Steven is driven by helping people and organisations succeed in their work and thrive career. Having put in the 'hard yards’ of bid writing throughout her career, she also knows that the Proposal industry is way overdue a ‘technological breakthrough’ - which is the reason she joined the leadership team at AutogenAI. With five kids, two snooty cats and one dopey Labrador, Tricia thrives on creativity, stimulation, and intravenous caffeine.

Fedor Klinkenberg - CEO & Co-Founder of Brainial
