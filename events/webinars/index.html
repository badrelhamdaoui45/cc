Free Education For APMP Members

Every month, APMP offers live webinars that address key industry topics and best practices. In addition to the live event, webinars are recorded and available for viewing at your convenience, providing you with an invaluable resource.

APMP webinars are perfect for obtaining CEUs. All content that is CEU/CPD eligible is labeled on the video card and in the video description at APMP TV. Each CEU/CPD eligible video must be manually recorded by you in your CEU/CPD tracker.

APMP webinars (live and recorded) are included in your membership.
