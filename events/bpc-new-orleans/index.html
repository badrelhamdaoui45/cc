Event Cancellation Policy

Registrations may be transferred to another individual at any time by contacting Member Services. We will honor transfers reported in advance.

If registration is canceled before April 8, 2024, we will refund the entire registration.

If a registration is canceled on or after April 8, 2024, we will refund 75% of the registration fee paid originally (Early Bird or Regular).

If registration is canceled on or after April 29, 2024, we will refund 50% of the registration fee paid originally (Early Bird or Regular).

If registration is canceled on or after May 17, 2024, there will be no refunds for cancellations. Again, registrations may be transferred to another individual at any time, provided we are notified beforehand.

APMP Certification Day | Sunday, June 2, 2024

Registration is separate from BPC New Orleans

APMP is offering a Foundation Level Certification Training and Exam at BPC New Orleans. This day-long intensive Foundation-level certification course includes lunch and will conclude with an in-person exam. This is available as an in-person offering only. At its completion, candidates sit the paper-based, multiple-choice exam and initial results (pass/fail) are delivered the same day. The testing organization will formally process and confirm official results via the APMG Candidate Portal within 2 working days of receipt of the exams.

Member Price: $760
Nonmember Price: $965

Time: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (includes the training, lunch and exam)

Exam Info: At the end of the training, candidates sit the paper-based, multiple-choice exam and initial results (pass/fail) are delivered the same day. The testing organization will formally process and confirm official results via the APMG Candidate Portal within two working days of receipt of the exams. It is recommended you purchase the Foundation Study Guide ($69 download for members) and Glossary ($25 download for members), as the Foundation exam is based on these resources.
