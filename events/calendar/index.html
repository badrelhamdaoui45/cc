BPC Barcelona 

BPC Barcelona 2024 offers two action-packed days of in-person events dedicated to professional development, networking, and expert industry education for professionals in the bid, proposal, tender, capture, sales, and business development fields.

During this event, you will gain valuable knowledge and skills that will enhance your job performance, allowing you to work more efficiently and effectively. We focus on providing you with the tools and insights to deliver value to your organization and customers through education, networking, and continuous learning.

Presented by the Association of Proposal Management Professionals (APMP), the only professional association in the industry with extensive size, depth, resources, and expertise to motivate winners in the business-winning community. BPC Barcelona 2024 is an opportunity to engage with and learn from the best of the business-winning community.
