APMP's mission is to be the trusted leader that serves an international community of bid and proposal development professionals.

The Association of Proposal Management Professionals is the internationally accepted authority serving and educating those who win business to drive revenue to their organization. It is the industry that serves all industries and promotes the professional growth of its members by advancing the arts, sciences, and technologies of bids and proposals.

APMP Certification Announcement

The Competitive Price To Win Micro-Certification is Here!

This fourth micro-certification assesses a candidate's understanding of the APMP Body of Knowledge, specifically in developing competitive pricing strategies for bid and proposal teams. Competitive Price To Win has become a major focus in the evolution of business development practices.

This Micro-Certification is more than just a credential; it is a mark of distinction in proposal management. It serves as a testament to your ability to contribute effectively to your team and the broader objectives of your organization.

We invite all professionals dedicated to enhancing their skills and contributing to their teams at the highest level to pursue their PW-M APMP!

Sponsored Webinar - February 1, 2024

Demystifying AI – Understanding How It Impacts Capture and Proposal Development

"Generative AI is not a replacement for human creativity, but rather a tool that can augment and enhance it.” -Sam Altman, CEO, OpenAI

Join us for an engaging discussion on AI & how it fits into Government Contracting. We’ll outline where AI applies in the Capture and Proposal development process & show how products (including VisibleThread) will evolve to automate many of these tasks using GenAI. We’ll cover the right and wrong use cases for GenAI, security & hallucinations, and what you need to know to use it well. This will be a practical, no-nonsense session with lots of useful takeaways.

Top Takeaways:

  • Understanding the best and worst use cases for GenAI
  • Security considerations when adopting AI in the enterprise
  • Where VisibleThread is adding GenAI & for what use cases

Applying the Proposal Leader Playbook With Microsoft and Optum

Microsoft and Optum have driven millions of dollars of impact by investing in their strategic response management programs. But it didn’t happen overnight. Hear how these proposal team leaders tackled team structure, content organization and executive visibility to unite multiple stakeholders around fast, winning responses that drove their business forward – with relevant applications for proposal teams of all sizes.

Top Takeaways:

  • Coping with the evolving and escalating pressures on proposal teams

Q&A with the APMP Professionals of Color Affinity Group Co-Chairs

As part of our ongoing commitment to nurturing an inclusive community of bid and proposal professionals, APMP has introduced a number of Affinity Groups, with the purpose of providing supportive spaces for professionals with shared experiences in life and the industry.

One of these groups is the Professionals of Color Affinity Group which, in 2024, will be headed up by Morgan Anderson, CF APMP and Jeremiah Walker, serving as the group’s co-chairs.

We sat down with Morgan and Jeremiah to discuss their journey towards leading this group, what the Affinity Group is all about, and their goals for 2024.
